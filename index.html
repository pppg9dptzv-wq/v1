      TrickMap for calisthenics

      This interactive tool helps you visualize and plan combos for your next competition, or just for fun

      How to use:

      • Search and add tricks from the left panel
      • Click on any trick to see compatible combinations
      • Drag and drop tricks to organize your workspace
      • Create connections and transitions between tricks
      • Click 'info' on any trick for an informative panel
      • Drag tricks to the trash icon to delete them
      • Click the trash to clear everything
